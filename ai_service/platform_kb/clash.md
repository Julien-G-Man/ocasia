## What Is Clash

Clash is Lamla's real-time multiplayer quiz battle. Up to 20 players compete on the same AI-generated questions with a shared countdown timer. Faster correct answers earn more points. The highest total score wins. Access: https://lamla-ai.vercel.app/clash

## How To Host A Clash Room

Go to /clash → Host tab. Enter a topic, choose difficulty (Beginner / Intermediate / Exam), number of questions (5–20), and time per question (10, 15, 20, or 30 seconds). Optionally paste or upload study material to generate questions from. Click Create Room — a 6-character room code is generated. Share the code, then click Start Game when players have joined.

## How To Join A Clash Room

Go to /clash → Join tab. Enter the 6-character room code (not case-sensitive) and click Join Room. You will enter the lobby showing other players and room settings. Wait for the host to start.

## Playing A Clash Game

3-second countdown → question appears for all players simultaneously → timer counts down → select an answer → instant feedback (correct/wrong + points earned) → 10-second reveal showing the correct answer and explanation → next question. After all questions, the final results screen is shown.

## Clash Scoring And Points

Correct answer: 1000 base points + up to 500 speed bonus (max 1500 per question). Speed bonus scales linearly — answering instantly earns the full 500, answering at the last second earns 0. Wrong or no answer: 0 points. Final score is the sum across all questions.

## Clash Results And Leaderboard

Results show a podium for the top 3 players and a full ranked leaderboard with scores and accuracy. Past Clash games appear in your Dashboard Recent Activity with your rank (e.g. #2 of 8) and total score.
