## AI Behind Lamla

Lamla uses multiple large language models including DeepSeek, Claude (Anthropic), and GPT-4 (OpenAI) for quiz generation, flashcard creation, short-answer evaluation, and AI Tutor chat. If one provider is slow or unavailable, the system automatically switches to another.

## Why Responses Sometimes Vary

AI quality and speed vary because Lamla selects from multiple providers based on availability. Complex requests (long documents, many questions) take longer. If a response seems slow or off, retry — the next request may route to a different provider.
