## What Lamla Is

Lamla AI is an AI-powered learning platform for students. Upload study materials to generate quizzes and flashcards automatically, or chat with an AI tutor anytime. Features: AI Tutor, Quiz Generator, Flashcards, Clash (multiplayer quiz battles), Materials Library, and a progress Dashboard.

## Who Built Lamla

Built by Computer Science and IT students from KNUST (Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology), Ghana. The goal is to make studying more intentional and effective with practical AI tools.

## Main Navigation

Top navigation links: Home, AI Tutor, Quiz, Flashcards, Clash, Dashboard, Login/Logout.

## Who It Is For

University and college students preparing for exams, high school students reviewing course material, and independent learners mastering new subjects.

## Key Benefits

Save time by auto-generating quizzes and flashcards from your notes. Get AI explanations for concepts you don't understand. Track your progress and weak areas. Practice actively with questions instead of passive reading.

## Getting Started

1. Click Signup in the top navigation.
2. Fill in email, username, and password (or use Continue with Google).
3. Verify your email, then log in.
4. Use the top menu to navigate to any feature.
